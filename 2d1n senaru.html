Climbing package - Expedition to the highest peak of Lombok Via Senaru

Duration: 2 Days 1 Night
Dificulty: Moderate
Price: Rp 2.000.000 per person

Description: Crater Rim Senaru Trekking Overview:

Pass grand tropical forest ( big trees and local strawberries), shadow trails Enjoy the jungle atmosphere with bird sounds, and wild animals ( black monkey, brown monkey, etc ) Spectacular view from of Gili’s, Senaru village and green tropical forest from Cemara Lima Unique Lake of Segara Anak and hotspring water Amazing view from crater RIM Senaru over Segara Anak view ( Sunset and Sunrise ).

Trekking itinerary:

Day 01. Breakfast at base camp Senaru, lunch, and dinner at the mountain.
  • Wake up at 6.00 AM and get breakfast
  • Prepare for trekking at 07.30 – 08.00 AM
  • Registration at the RTC ( Rinjani Trekking Centre Senaru 601m )
  • Walking in the open views for 30 minutes with green plants of coffee trees, Bamboo, etc
  • Pass walk 30 minutes more to the gate of the tropical rain forest, 15 minutes short break
  • Continue walking through the jungle where we will shadowing by the sun for 3 hours to POS 2 ( MONTONG SATAS 1.500m ), seeing black monkeys and big trees on the trail.
  • Break at POS 2, prepare fresh fruits and soft drinks by our Guide and porter, lunch will prepare at POS 3 ( Fresh fruits, snacks/biscuits,s and lunch )
  • Continue to POS 3 ( MONDOKAN LOLAK 2.000m ) pass tropical forest, local strawberry ( June, July, August ), 3 hours walk.
  • continue to crater Rim of Senaru ( 2.641m ) one hour walk
  • Camping and overnight at crater RIM Senaru, enjoy an amazing sunset over 3 Gili’s and Mount Agung Bali
#Overview trail on day 1:
  • Distance: 8 km
  • Walk duration: 6-7 hours
  • Difficulty: Moderate
  • Highest point: 2,641 m ( Plawangan Senaru crater rim )
  • Height gain: +2,016 m
Day 02. Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner at the Mountain.
  • Breakfast and enjoy sunrise by the beautiful scenery of Segara Anak Lake
  • Walk down to Senaru village for 4-5 hours
  • Will arrive at Senaru village at approx 13.00 PM
  • Prepare your luggage, then transfer to your next destination ( Senggigi, Mataram, or Bangsal Harbor)
